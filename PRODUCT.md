# Product

## Register

product

## Users
Thai retail stock investors, ranging from beginners (who need intuitive layouts, clear financial explanations, and simple visual cues) to professional investors (who need advanced screeners, DCF simulation sandboxes, and detailed tabular data).

## Product Purpose
To provide a clear, easy-to-use, yet highly powerful stock screener and valuation platform. The stocks page serves as the entry point to search, filter, and simulate stock valuations using models like DCF and Graham Number.

## Brand Personality
Professional, trustworthy, clean, and empowering. It should feel like a premium financial terminal that is accessible to all, avoiding overly technical jargon without sacrificing data depth.

## Anti-references
- Cluttered, overwhelming spreadsheets without visual cues.
- Over-simplified, cartoonish designs that lack credibility or look like simple toy apps.
- Dark terminals that are hard to read due to low contrast.

## Design Principles
- **Clarity Over Complexity**: Default to simple, visually striking card views for beginners, and allow seamless transitions to high-density tables for pros.
- **Progressive Disclosure**: Show high-level insights first (e.g. undervalued/overvalued labels, simple MOS bars), and reveal advanced parameters (DCF growth rates, discount rates) on demand or in Pro mode.
- **Visual Confidence**: Use clear colors (e.g. green for safety/undervalued, red for overvalued) with high contrast to make indicators readable at a glance.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- High text-to-background contrast (>= 4.5:1).
- Clear, readable typography (IBM Plex Sans Thai for Thai text, Outfit/Inter for numbers).
- Accessible interactive controls and clear focus states.
